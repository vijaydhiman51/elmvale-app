Welcome to Elmvale Horticultural Society

Educating and promoting horticulture, protecting the environment, and beautifying our community.

Our Mission

The Elmvale Horticultural Society is a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting all branches of horticulture. We encourage the planting of trees, shrubs, and flowers in our communities and advocate for the protection of the environment through educational programs and public events.

Volunteer with Us

We are always looking for passionate volunteers to help with our events, plantings, and community outreach programs. Volunteering with the Elmvale Horticultural Society is a great way to get involved in your community and make a lasting impact.

Become a Member

By becoming a member of the Elmvale Horticultural Society, you gain access to exclusive events, workshops, and discounts. Your support helps us continue our mission of beautifying Elmvale and educating the public on environmental sustainability.
